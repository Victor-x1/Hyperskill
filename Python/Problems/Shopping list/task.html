Shopping list Open on JetBrains Academy

Ruth goes shopping today, so she is busy making a shopping list.

She wrote down things that she would like to buy at the grocery store: first "milk", then "olive oil" and "bananas". When sneaking a look at the fridge, she noticed that there was some milk left and changed her mind about buying a new one. It got crossed out of the list. And, finally, a "brownie" became the last item Ruth had put on the list.

Now you try to replicate these operations on the variable shopping_list.

You are NOT supposed to print the results.

You can add an item with list.append() and delete it with list.remove().
